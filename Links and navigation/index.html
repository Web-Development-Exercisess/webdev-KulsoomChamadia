Overview

Peter Parker's secret identity is revealed, leading him to seek help from Doctor Strange. But when the spell goes wrong, visitors from other universes arrive, bringing chaos.

Key Information

Release Date: December 17, 2021. Production: Marvel Studios & Sony Pictures. Genre: Superhero, Action, Adventure.

Cast & Creative Team

Director: Jon Watts. Stars: Tom Holland, Zendaya, Benedict Cumberbatch, Alfred Molina, Willem Dafoe. Writers: Chris McKenna, Erik Sommers.

Characters

Peter Parker/Spider-Man: A young hero battling threats from across universes. MJ: Peter's girlfriend and ally. Doctor Strange: Master of the Mystic Arts.

Reviews

‘A multiverse-sized triumph.’ – Variety. ‘Spider-Man: No Way Home is pure cinematic magic.’ – IGN.
