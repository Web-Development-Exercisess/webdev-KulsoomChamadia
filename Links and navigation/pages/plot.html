Plot Summary

Red Dead Redemption 2 follows Arthur Morgan, a rugged outlaw and a member of the Van der Linde gang, as he struggles with loyalty, morality, and survival in a rapidly changing world. Set in 1899, the game tells a gripping story about the fall of the Old West and the people clinging to its fading way of life.
