Chocolate Fudge Brownies!

With their soft texture and deep chocolate flavor, these brownies are the perfect treat for making any gathering a little more special.

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
    2. Line an 8-inch square baking pan with parchment paper (so your brownies don’t stick and betray you).
    3. In a double boiler (or heatproof bowl over simmering water), melt the butter and chocolate.
    4. Remove from heat, and whisk in sugar.
    5. Whisk in eggs, one at a time, until fully combined.
    6. Mix in cocoa powder and salt. Stir like it's a workout.
    7. Fold in flour until just combined. No overmixing!
    8. Pour the batter into your prepared pan and spread it evenly.
    9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with moist crumbs (not wet batter!).
    10. Let them cool in the pan for about 15 minutes. Then cut them into squares and enjoy your homemade chocolate masterpiece.

    Please Note: I hope you enjoy these brownies, and once you’ve tasted them, you won’t be able to stop making them.
