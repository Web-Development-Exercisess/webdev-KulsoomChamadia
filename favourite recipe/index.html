Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It’s quick, creamy, and packed with flavour — perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Method

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and mix in the parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some of the cooking water.
  5. Quickly toss the spaghetti with the pancetta and remove from heat.
  6. Stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding a little cooking water if needed to make it creamy.
  7. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
